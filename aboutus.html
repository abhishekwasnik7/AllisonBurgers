Who Are We ?

What happens when an IT professional and a Dictator come together? A homegrown burger chain is born.

Abhishek Wasnik and AD. General Aladeen founded Allison Burger's in 2023 to offer Indian consumers a taste of fusion burgers in a market dominated by US fast food QSR chains. Wat-a-Burger’s desi recipes are suited for Indian palettes. The emphasis is on original Indian recipes and not tweaking existing flavours. Pudina chutney, Achari flavour, Makhani sauce, Spicy chilly are some of our key offerings.

Starting a business was always on Abhishek and Aladeen’s minds. While Abhishek was in Pune pursuing his MCA and Aladeen was undergoing Dictator training, both knew that once they were back in Nagpur, they would set up a business together.

Since opening their first outlet in Noida Sector 18, Wat-a-Burger has scaled to more than 65 joints across 27 cities in over One years. This includes Nagpur,Delhi, Bengaluru, Ahmedabad, Mumbai, Guwahati, Lucknow, Patna, Ranchi, Muzaffarpur, Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Gorakhpur, Jhansi, Kozhikode, and Gurgaon to name a few.
